WarmGlow Living

WarmGlow Living is a user-friendly e-commerce web application designed for a seamless home-goods shopping experience. It features secure user authentication with registration and login, a dynamic product catalog with category filtering and search, and an interactive cart with purchase tracking. Customers can view product details, confirm orders with delivery addresses, and review their past purchases in a dedicated section. The platform is built with responsive design for accessibility across devices, ensuring smooth navigation and elegant visuals — combining convenience, style, and functionality for local store customers both online and in-store.
